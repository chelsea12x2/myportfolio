Yayoi Kusama Exhibition

These two posters are the product of an assignment for an Introduction to Studio Art class in which I had to create two posters in different styles for an imaginary exhibition of an artist whose work I would have liked to see.

Peace of Mind (Mental Health Awareness Club)

In high school, my friend and I founded a mental health awareness club called Peace of Mind. These are posters I designed for events we hosted during mental health awareness month. (Designed using Canva.)
